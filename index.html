POS first.
Website second.

On our call, Jordan said: "If we don't have the systems in place, the website really wouldn't be quite as effective." Agreed. Without clean inventory and a real POS, the website would inherit the same gaps you already see. Here's the phased plan.

✓ Phase 1 — Priority

Replace Profit Systems with Shopify POS

Target: First of the year (early 2027)

Get the operational backbone right. New POS, barcoded tags, iPads on the sales floor, live inventory, and integrations with QuickBooks, Synchrony, Acima, Snap Finance & Podium.

  • POS Pro live at $67/mo (vs. $1,200/mo Profit Systems)
  • Free migration partner handles products, images, inventory, customers, gift cards, loyalty
  • Staff trained alongside Profit Systems — no downtime
  • iPad mobility for sales floor immediately
  • Customer data and order history captured from day one
Phase 2 — Next

Launch a curated Shopify storefront

Target: 60–90 days after POS go-live

With clean inventory data flowing, the website becomes a real tool. Show only what you carry, with live stock visibility, financing at checkout, and the Podium chat you already love.

  • The "Goldilocks" middle ground Denise described — curated, not 300 pages of dressers
  • Live stock visibility & in-store availability filter
  • Synchrony / Acima / Snap Finance at checkout
  • Warranty + delivery options at checkout
  • Podium chat + review automation integrated

On the generational change

Denise put it well on our call — change is hard in a 66-year family business. We won't ask Dad or the cousin to learn five new systems at once. Phase 1 keeps the workflows familiar — same sales process, same pricing flexibility — just on better tools. Phase 2 only kicks off once everyone is comfortable. The goal is modernization without dislocation.

03 — Workflow

The day-to-day,
handwritten ticket to fulfillment

Here's what a sale looks like on Shopify POS — without losing the salesperson-customer relationship you've built the business on.

01

Customer walks in. Salesperson grabs an iPad.

No more "let me go check the back" or running to the terminal. The salesperson stays with the customer, with full inventory visibility in their hand — including what's in the showroom, the warehouse, and what's available to order from Basset, Ashley, etc.

✦ Shopify POS on iPad — included in POS Pro
02

Customer picks the couch. Price flexes when needed.

Salesperson scans the barcode or selects the SKU. The list price shows, but staff with permission can apply a price override — preserving the family business pricing flexibility your team values. All logged for reporting.

✦ Native price overrides with staff permissions
03

Add financing, warranty, and delivery — at the iPad.

Customer wants Synchrony? Apply at the POS, get approved on-screen. Want the extended warranty? Toggle it on. Delivery scheduled? Capture address, pick window, done — all in one transaction.

✦ Synchrony / Acima / Snap Finance app integrations
04

Sale closes. No handwritten ticket.

Transaction processes once. Inventory updates live across POS and the eventual website. Customer profile saved for future visits, marketing, and review request via Podium. Sam doesn't re-enter anything.

✦ One entry — no double work for Sam
05

Podium sends a review request. QuickBooks gets the data.

Behind the scenes, Podium fires off a review invite (you keep your existing positive review flywheel). QuickBooks Online syncs the sale into your books automatically. No new workflows for your team — just the old ones, done better.

✦ Podium + QuickBooks Online — both integrate with Shopify

Inventory

Live stock across showroom, attic & web

Dedicate inventory to specific locations (showroom floor, attic warehouse, online-only). The POS shows live counts; the website shows real availability. Annual hand-count becomes a continuous, automated process.

Print barcode tags directly from Shopify. Sales scan instead of handwriting tickets. Staff with permission can still apply price overrides for negotiated deals — the way Dad and the cousin always have — but it's now logged.

Website

Curated catalog with in-stock filter

The "Goldilocks" version Denise asked for. Show what you actually have or can quickly get — not 300 pages of vendor catalog. Customers filter by "in store today" or "order from manufacturer." Each product shows real availability.

Synchrony, Acima, and Snap Finance all integrate at checkout. Warranties can be offered as add-ons. Delivery vs. pickup is selectable, with address capture and date scheduling. All native to Shopify checkout.
